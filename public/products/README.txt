PUT NEW PRODUCT PHOTOS IN THIS FOLDER.

Example: if you save a photo here named  my-sofa.jpg
then in src/data/products.ts you write:

  images: ["/products/my-sofa.jpg"]

Tips:
- Use simple file names: small letters, numbers and dashes. No spaces.
  Good: brown-sofa-1.jpg   Bad: My New Sofa (1).JPG
- .jpg, .png and .webp all work. .webp loads fastest.

Full instructions: PRODUCT_MANAGEMENT_GUIDE.md in the project root.
